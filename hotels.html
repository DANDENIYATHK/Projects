Talalla Leisure..


    Property description:-


Situated in Talalla, Talalla Leisure offers beachfront accommodation 600 m from Talalla Beach and features various facilities, such as a garden, a restaurant and a private beach area. The property is set less than 1 km from Edanda Wella Beach, a 13-minute walk from Kapparawella Beach and 15 km from Hummanaya Blow Hole. The accommodation offers room service and an ATM for guests. At the hotel, rooms have a balcony. The private bathroom is fitted with a shower and free toiletries. The rooms at Talalla Leisure have air conditioning and a wardrobe. Buffet and continental breakfast options are available each morning at the accommodation. Weherahena Buddhist Temple is 9.3 km from Talalla Leisure, while Kushtarajagala is 30 km from the property. The nearest airport is Koggala Airport, 44 km from the hotel.
